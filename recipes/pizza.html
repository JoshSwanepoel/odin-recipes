Pizza

Description

This simple brick-oven pizza recipe has been made famous by several well-known, Brooklyn wood-fire pizzerias. Best accompanied by ice-cold, pilsner-style lager beer.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Bread Flour
  • Dry Yeast
  • Salt
  • Mozerella Cheese
  • Oregano
  • Pepper
  • Basil
  • Canned Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Mix yeast, warm water, and sugar. Let it foam.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Knead until smooth. Let it rise for 1-2 hours.
  3. Heat canned tomatoes with olive oil and oregano. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Roll out the dough. Add sauce, cheese, and toppings.
  5. Bake at high heat until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
